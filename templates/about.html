About This Portal

The Marksheet Management Portal is designed to provide students and faculty with a digital platform to access and manage academic records efficiently. It ensures transparency, quick access, and secure storage of marksheets.

Built using Flask, HTML, CSS, and SQLite, this portal demonstrates a full-stack web development approach suitable for college-level projects and internship work.

Our Mission

To empower students and educators by simplifying the process of result management, making academic data accessible, reliable, and secure for everyone.

Key Highlights

  • Instant access to marksheets and GPA for every exam
  • Downloadable PDF marksheets for easy sharing
  • Admin dashboard for efficient management of records and notifications
  • Secure login and data encryption for privacy
  • Result history and analytics for academic tracking
  • Responsive design for use on any device

Who Can Use This Portal?

  • Students: To view, download, and track their academic performance
  • Faculty: To manage student records and publish results
  • Administrators: To oversee the entire academic record system
